What is The People's Market Dashboard System?

The People's Market Dashboard System (PMDS) is a customized information technology platform developed for the Multnomah County Health Department REACH program (REACH) and PlayGrowLearn (PGL), a non-profit community farm organization that runs the people's market. It integrates secure data collection, real-time dashboards, and automated reporting to improve market operations, data management, and fiscal transactions. The PMDS was designed and developed by the Homegrown Community and Technology Design Lab at the University of Cincinnati School of Public and International Affairs with support from the University of Cincinnati School of Information Technology.



The goals of the PMDS are to 1 build administration and data management capacity among the organizations participating in the People's Market, and 2) increase the effective implementation of the People's Market and the attainment of Market goals established by REACH and PGL. The PMDS addresses key challenges identified by year 1 Market participants by facilitating data validity, process and logistical management, information transparency, data-driven decision-making, and impact measurement.
